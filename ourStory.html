Hello! Welcome to the Sip Sisters' world!

Sip Sisters, founded in 2003 by two women in a charming Bulgarian town, aimed to establish a place where people of all genders could feel comfortable and have fun. One of the pivotal moments came in 2006 when they ventured into brewing.

The creativity of the team outgrowed the limits of the initial shop, leading them to not only they change the building, but to rent another one in other city - Plovdiv. The first achievment for this little family shop came in 2010, when winning the Innovation Award for introducing a unique and experimental line of beers, showcasing their commitment to the brewing industry. The brewery made a bold move by introducing degustation sessions, offering a platform for enthusiasts to savor authentic beers inspired by Germany, Belgium, the Czech Republic, the Netherlands and the USA.
With a dedicated team of around 50 individuals—comprising brewers, managers, and IT professionals — Sip Sisters stands as a testament to the abilities and talents that drive their success.
