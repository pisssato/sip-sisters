Here are the answers to some questions you might have, before coming to our degustation session:

How long are the sessions and when are they happening?

Our beer degustation sessions are designed to accommodate your schedule, taking place every day, including weekends from 18:00 to 20:00. Each session typically lasts an hour or two hours, providing you with a perfect opportunity to immerse yourself in the world of craft beer. Whether you're a weekday enthusiast or prefer weekend indulgence, our daily sessions ensure you can enjoy the experience at your convenience.

What is the cost of a session?

The cost per person for our beer degustation sessions is from 50.00 to 70.00 lv. We believe in fostering a sense of community and sharing the joy of beer exploration with friends and family. Therefore, we offer enticing group discounts for gatherings of more participants. It's a fantastic way to enhance the experience by enjoying the flavors together. Click at our pricelist to choose your best deal:

Is prior reservation required for the session?

Yes, it is highly recommended to make a prior reservation for our beer degustation sessions. This ensures that we can provide you with the best experience, adequate seating, and personalized service. Reservations can be made through our online booking system or by contacting our customer service.

Can we customize the beer selection for our group?

To maintain an element of surprise and ensure a diverse experience, our beer degustation sessions come with a curated selection that caters to various tastes. While customization might not be available for individual preferences, rest assured that our selection is crafted to offer something special for everyone in your group. We look forward to sharing an array of flavors that will leave a lasting impression on your beer-tasting adventure!
